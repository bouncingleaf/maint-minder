About

Maint Minder was written by Jessica M. Roy for Boston University's MET CS 701, Rich Internet Applications. It uses:

  • Angular and RxJS.
  • VMWare's Project Clarity for UI.
  • CarQueryAPI.com for car make and model information.
  • ArcGIS World Geocoding Service for the service location field (i.e., where the work is being done).
  • Google Maps geocoding for helping determine the user's own location.
