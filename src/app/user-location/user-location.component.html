My Location

When you tell Maint Minder about work that was done on your vehicle, you can enter the location where the work was done, such as a repair shop or car dealer. If Maint Minder knows where you are, it can offer better suggestions for places local to you.

Unfortunately, your browser doesn't support geolocation. You'll still be able to enter place names, Maint Minder just can't help narrow it down.
